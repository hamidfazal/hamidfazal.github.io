Clinical Philosophy

The foundation of my practice

Effective healthcare requires treating the body and the mind as a single unit. My objective is to apply global medical standards—validated by my MD certification and US research background—to local community health challenges. I specialize in the integration of metabolic medicine and psychiatric care, providing evidence-based, compassionate care to populations where comorbid physical and mental illnesses intersect.
